College Costs & Loans

Plan for tuition, financial aid, and ways to save for your education to avoid unnecessary debt.

Tuition & Fees

Understand the difference between in-state, out-of-state, and private school costs.

Financial Aid

  • Grants: free money based on need or merit
  • Scholarships: merit or need-based awards
  • Work-study programs: part-time work to help pay for college

Student Loans

Borrow only what you need. Compare interest rates and repayment plans before taking loans.

Budgeting for College

  • Track your spending on books, food, and entertainment
  • Use student discounts and free resources
  • Start saving before college to reduce loan dependency
