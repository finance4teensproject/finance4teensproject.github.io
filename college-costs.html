College Costs & Loans

Plan ahead for college tuition, loans, and ways to save for your education.

Lessons & Activities

Tuition & Fees

Consider tuition, housing, meals, and supplies when budgeting for college.

Student Loans

Loans help pay for school but must be repaid with interest. Federal loans usually have lower interest than private ones.

Ways to Save

  • Scholarships and grants
  • Part-time jobs
  • Early savings

Practical Exercises

  • Calculate a sample college budget.
  • Research 3 scholarships you could apply for.
  • Compare two loan options for cost differences.

Quiz Yourself

Mini-quiz: Which loan is cheaper? How can scholarships reduce borrowing?
