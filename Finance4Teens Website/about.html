About The Project

This Gold Award project is focused on helping teens understand personal finance. We provide easy-to-read resources, interactive quizzes, and workshops to teach essential money skills.

Project Goals

  • Teach teens how to budget, save, and spend wisely.
  • Provide clear information about credit, debt, and loans.
  • Offer interactive learning tools like quizzes and workshops.
  • Support financial literacy in a teen-friendly way.
