What You'll Learn

Budgeting & Saving

  • Create a realistic budget.
  • Set SMART financial goals.
  • Build healthy saving habits.
  • Understand emergency funds.

Credit

  • Understand credit scores.
  • Read a credit report.
  • Use credit responsibly.
  • Avoid common mistakes.

Interest & Debt

  • Simple vs. compound interest.
  • How debt grows.
  • Strategies to avoid debt.
  • Borrowing wisely.

College Costs

  • FAFSA basics.
  • Scholarships & grants.
  • Student loans.
  • Budgeting during college.

Before You Begin

This course is designed for middle and high school students with little or no prior financial knowledge. Read each lesson carefully, complete the activities, and take your time with the quizzes. There is no time limit, and every lesson builds on the previous one.

Ready to Begin?

Start by taking the pre-assessment. Once you're finished, return here and begin Lesson 1: Budgeting & Saving.
