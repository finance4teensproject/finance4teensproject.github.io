Interest & Debt

Learn how interest works, how debt accumulates, and how to manage it responsibly.

What is Interest?

Interest is the cost of borrowing money, usually expressed as a percentage of the loan.

Types of Debt

  • Credit card debt
  • Student loans
  • Auto loans
  • Personal loans

Strategies to Manage Debt

  • Pay more than the minimum when possible
  • Prioritize high-interest debt first
  • Create a repayment plan

Practice Example

If you owe $500 on a credit card with 18% APR, paying only $25/month will take longer and cost more than paying $50/month.
