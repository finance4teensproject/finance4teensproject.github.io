Interest & Debt

Understanding interest and managing debt is critical for avoiding financial stress. Learn how borrowing costs work and how to pay off debt wisely.

Core Lessons

What is Interest?

Interest is the cost of borrowing money. Lenders charge interest on loans or credit cards.

Types of Debt

Good debt (like student loans) can help your future, while bad debt (high-interest credit cards) can be harmful.

Paying Off Debt

Pay more than the minimum if you can. Focus on high-interest debt first to save money in the long term.

Examples & Scenarios

  • Sam has a $1000 credit card balance at 20% interest. Paying only minimums will take years to repay.
  • Jade uses a student loan for college. Interest is lower and manageable if paid carefully.

Activities & Practice

1. Calculate interest: $500 borrowed at 12% annual interest. How much is owed after 1 year?

2. Scenario: Decide which debt to pay off first: $2000 at 15% vs $500 at 8%.
