Interest & Debt

Understand interest, manage debt wisely, and avoid financial stress.

Lessons & Activities

What is Interest?

Interest is the cost of borrowing money. Lenders charge interest on loans or credit cards.

Types of Debt

Good debt (student loans) can help your future. Bad debt (high-interest credit cards) can cause problems.

Paying Off Debt

Pay more than the minimum if possible. Focus on high-interest debt first to save money over time.

Practical Exercises

  • Calculate interest on $500 at 12% annual rate.
  • Decide which debt to pay off first: $2000 at 15% vs $500 at 8% interest.

Quiz Yourself

Mini-quiz: How does paying only the minimum affect debt? Which debts cost more over time?
