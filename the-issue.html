The Issue

Many teens don’t learn how to manage money in school. This leaves them unprepared for real-life expenses, debt, and saving for the future.

Why Financial Literacy Matters

Understanding money early helps teens make better choices, avoid unnecessary debt, and save for their goals.

Resources

Financial Literacy Statistics

Teens who learn money skills are more likely to save and avoid debt.

Common Pitfalls

Credit card debt, student loans, and overspending are major issues.

Why it’s hard to learn

Schools often don’t teach personal finance, so teens rely on trial and error.
