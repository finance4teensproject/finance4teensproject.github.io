Budgeting & Saving

Learn how to manage your money, save smart, and reach your goals with real-life examples and interactive activities.

Lessons & Activities

What is Budgeting?

Create a plan for your money to track income and spending. Start simple: income - needs - wants - savings.

Short-Term vs Long-Term Goals

Short-term: saving for a new game or gift. Long-term: saving for college or a car. Both need planning!

Needs vs Wants

Learn to prioritize essentials like food, housing, and transportation over wants like shopping or streaming subscriptions.

Practical Exercises

  • Create a weekly budget based on your allowance or income.
  • Track your spending for one week and see where you can save.
  • Set one short-term and one long-term financial goal.

Quiz Yourself

Try a mini-quiz: What’s a need? How much should you save each month? Use your budget to check your answers.
