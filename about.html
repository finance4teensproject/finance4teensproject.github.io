About Finance 4 Teens

A teen-led platform created to empower students with practical money skills for life.

Our Mission

Finance 4 Teens exists to help young people build confidence in managing money. From budgeting and saving to understanding credit and loans, our resources are designed to be relatable, interactive, and practical for everyday life.

About the Gold Award Project

The Girl Scout Gold Award is the highest achievement in Girl Scouting. It recognizes leadership, community impact, and initiative. For my Gold Award project, I created Finance 4 Teens to give teens the tools they need to make smart financial decisions, bridging the gap in financial education that many schools do not cover.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Short lessons on budgeting, credit, interest, debt, and college costs.
  • Interactive quizzes and activities to test your knowledge.
  • Resources, infographics, and workshops for practical learning.
