Our Mission

Financial decisions begin long before adulthood. Finance 4 Teens helps students develop practical money skills they can use now and throughout their lives. Through lessons, workshops, quizzes, and real-world examples, our goal is to make financial education engaging, accessible, and easy to understand.

About This Project

Finance 4 Teens was created as a Girl Scout Gold Award project to address the lack of accessible financial education available to middle and high school students. Through free lessons, workshops, and resources, the project aims to equip students with practical money skills they can use throughout life.
