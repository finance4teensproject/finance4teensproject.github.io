Credit

Learn how credit works, how to build a strong score, and how to avoid common mistakes.

Lessons & Activities

What is Credit?

Credit lets you borrow money now and pay it back later. Credit cards, loans, and lines of credit are common examples.

Credit Scores

Your score shows how responsible you are with money. Pay on time, keep balances low, and manage accounts wisely.

Good vs Bad Credit Habits

  • Good: Paying bills on time, low balances, responsible borrowing.
  • Bad: Missing payments, maxing out cards, borrowing more than you can repay.

Practical Exercises

  • Check your spending habits and imagine a credit card scenario.
  • Create a mock budget showing how you would pay off a $200 balance.

Quiz Yourself

Mini-quiz: What action improves credit? What hurts it? Test yourself using your examples.
