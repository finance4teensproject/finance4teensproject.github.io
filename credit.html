Credit

Understanding credit is essential for borrowing money responsibly and building financial independence.

What is Credit?

Credit allows you to borrow money now and pay it back later. It can be used for things like credit cards, loans, or buying a car.

Credit Scores

Your credit score reflects your borrowing reliability. Key factors include:

  • Payment history
  • Credit utilization
  • Length of credit history
  • Types of credit accounts

Responsible Borrowing

  • Only borrow what you can pay back
  • Make payments on time
  • Keep balances low on credit cards

Practice Example

Example: If you charge $100 on a credit card and pay $50 this month, you maintain credit usage at 50%, which helps your score.
